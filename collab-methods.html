合作模式範例Engagement Models

以下為常見合作模式的範例，我們樂於討論符合您需求的客製化結構Examples of common engagement models; we welcome discussion of custom structures

模式 A：服務付費Model A: Fee-for-Service
Fee-for-Service / Transactional

適合需要快速解決特定問題的客戶。我們交付優化序列，您進行驗證。這是一個高效率、低複雜度的交易型合作模式，適合一次性專案或初步探索。 For clients who need fast solutions to specific problems. We deliver optimized sequences; you validate. Efficient, low-complexity transactional model for one-off or exploratory projects.

  • 明確的專案範圍與交付物Clear scope and deliverables
  • 固定時程與價格Fixed timeline and price
  • 快速交付與驗證Fast delivery and validation

模式 B：迭代共同開發Model B: Iterative Co-Development
Iterative Co-Development / The Feedback Loop

更深入的合作夥伴關係，客戶的實驗數據持續回饋至我們的引擎，讓我們能夠針對特定目標持續精進模型。透過多輪迭代，預測準確度顯著提升。 Deeper partnership: your experimental data feeds back into our engine so we can refine the model for your targets. Multiple rounds of iteration significantly improve prediction accuracy.

  • 實驗數據回饋循環Experimental data feedback loop
  • 持續模型優化Ongoing model optimization
  • 針對性改進設計Targeted design improvements

模式 C：聯合研發 / 策略聯盟Model C: Joint R&D / Strategic Alliance
Joint R&D / Strategic Alliance

針對高價值、高複雜度的目標，我們與客戶共同承擔風險與共享成果。適合需要長期投入、技術突破或共同探索新領域的專案。 For high-value, high-complexity goals we share risk and outcomes with the client. Suited to long-term commitment, technical breakthroughs, or exploring new areas together.

  • 風險與成果共享Shared risk and outcomes
  • 深度技術整合Deep technical integration
  • 長期戰略合作Long-term strategic collaboration

重要說明：Note: 以上模式僅為範例，並非完整列表。我們歡迎討論創新的合作結構、學術合作框架，或混合式模式，以最符合您的需求。每一項合作都是獨特的，我們將與您共同設計最適合的安排。 These models are examples only. We welcome discussion of innovative structures, academic collaboration frameworks, or hybrid models to best fit your needs. Every collaboration is unique; we will design the right arrangement with you.

靈活智慧財產權架構Flexible IP Framework

智慧財產權條款可協商且依合作模式量身訂製IP terms are negotiable and tailored to the engagement model

智慧財產權條款是任何合作協議的核心，我們理解不同的專案與商業目標需要不同的 IP 結構。我們的 IP 安排是可協商的，並會根據選擇的合作模式進行量身訂製。 IP terms are at the core of any collaboration. We understand that different projects and business goals need different IP structures. Our IP arrangements are negotiable and tailored to the chosen engagement model.

服務模式（Service Model）Service Model

通常採用客戶完整擁有優化序列所有權的架構。Typically, the client retains full ownership of optimized sequences.

共同開發（Joint Development）Joint Development

可能採用共享智慧財產權（Shared IP）的架構，具體分配比例在專案協議中明確規定。May use a shared IP structure; allocation is specified in the project agreement.

其他結構Other structures

我們開放討論授權模式、里程碑付款結構，或其他符合您商業目標的 IP 安排。We are open to licensing, milestone payments, or other IP arrangements that fit your business goals.

所有智慧財產權的具體條款將在專案開始前的協議中詳細規定，確保雙方權益清晰且受到法律保障。 All IP terms will be set out in the agreement before the project starts, ensuring clear rights and legal protection for both parties.
