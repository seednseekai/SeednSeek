我們是誰Who We Are

SeednSeek 是一支跨學科研究團隊，匯聚醫學科學、物理學與資訊工程領域的專家研究員。我們專注於解決蛋白質工程中的「結構-功能」難題，運用專有生成式 AI 引擎為製藥公司、生物技術企業與研究機構提供專業的計算蛋白質優化服務。 SeednSeek is an interdisciplinary research team bringing together experts in medical science, physics, and information engineering. We focus on solving the structure–function challenge in protein engineering, using our proprietary generative AI engine to provide computational protein optimization for pharmaceutical companies, biotech firms, and research institutions.

我們的團隊成員在計算生物學、結構生物學、蛋白質工程與深度學習領域擁有豐富的研發經驗，致力於將前沿研究成果轉化為實際的應用解決方案。 Our team has extensive R&D experience in computational biology, structural biology, protein engineering, and deep learning, and we are committed to turning cutting-edge research into practical solutions.

我們的理念Our Philosophy

我們堅信「計算優先」（In-Silico First）策略是蛋白質優化的未來。傳統的濕實驗室方法需要數月甚至數年的迭代循環，消耗大量時間與資源。透過先進的計算設計，我們能在數天內生成數千種優化候選序列，大幅減少實驗浪費並加速研發進程。 We believe an In-Silico First strategy is the future of protein optimization. Traditional wet-lab approaches require months or years of iteration and consume significant time and resources. With advanced computational design, we can generate thousands of optimized candidate sequences in days, reducing experimental waste and accelerating R&D.

我們的平台結合分子動力學模擬，能夠處理現成軟體無法應對的複雜、非標準約束條件，為客戶提供真正客製化的優化方案。 Our platform combines molecular dynamics simulation to handle complex, non-standard constraints that off-the-shelf software cannot address, delivering truly customized optimization for our clients.

技術平台Technology Platform

專有生成式 AI 驅動的體外演化系統Proprietary generative AI–driven in vitro evolution

專有生成式 AI 引擎Generative AI Engine

我們開發的高維度神經計算平台，整合深度學習與生成式模型，能夠在廣闊的序列空間中探索並生成優化變體。引擎經過大量蛋白質結構與功能資料訓練，能夠理解複雜的序列-結構-功能關係。 Our high-dimensional neural platform combines deep learning and generative models to explore sequence space and generate optimized variants, trained on structure and function data to capture sequence–structure–function relationships.

體外演化平台In Vitro Evolution

我們的體外演化平台模擬自然選擇過程，在定義的約束條件下迭代優化序列。結合多目標優化演算法，能夠同時處理多個競爭性目標，找到最佳平衡點。 Our in vitro evolution platform simulates natural selection to iteratively optimize sequences under defined constraints, with multi-objective optimization to balance competing goals.

分子動力學驗證MD Validation

每個候選序列都經過分子動力學模擬驗證，評估結構穩定性、摺疊動力學與功能表現。這確保我們交付的序列不僅在序列空間中優化，更在物理結構層面具有可信度。 Every candidate is validated by molecular dynamics for stability, folding, and function—ensuring sequences are not only optimized in sequence space but structurally credible.

持續學習機制Continuous Learning

基於客戶反饋與實驗驗證結果，我們的平台持續學習與改進。匿名化的成功案例數據（經客戶同意）用於提升模型性能，但絕不用於訓練公開模型或洩露客戶智慧財產權。 Feedback and validation data drive continuous improvement. Anonymized success cases (with consent) improve our models but are never used to train public models or expose client IP.
